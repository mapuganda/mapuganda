Our Background

OPEN STREET MAP UGANDA was founded in 2012 with a vision to create a GIS awareness through promoting OpenStreet Map and Open data and creating a network of resilient mappers that could tackle the global sustainable goals.

Mapping activities would be done and various communities were engaged until the Organisation was officially registered as an NGO in 2016 and received a certificate of Incorporation in April 2017.

A board was formed which has been running till now. At end 2017 Map Uganda was embraced into a World Bank consortium for an OSM project with lead Organisation ITHICA in Italy. Another opportunity found its place in our Organisation for Smart Cities Project Funded SOM-Africa. In January 2018, a functional organisation was put in place with an ED, 2 Project Managers, Mapping and training Officer and a GIS Assistant in order to fulfil project requirements and expand the business. Several other projects have been carried out before in conjunction with other entities like mapping for financial inclusion, Where is my transport and the highly appreciated State of The Map Africa event.
